Create an interactive web visualization of the Randomized Numerical Linear Algebra Research community. 
To begin, you can use the people who attended the Banff and ICERM workshops, and then add anyone who may be missing.

Nodes are people, and edges indicate how close they are.
I want to be able to click into nodes and edges to get more information.
I am not sure of the metric for this yet, so make it general. 
We may want to tune it based on some combination of factors.

While it will be good for this approach we use to be general so that we can update it / extend it to other communities, the RandNLA community is relatively small, so some stuff can be created directly by you rather than done programatically, if it makes sense.

Think about how to approach this problem, how to obtain and organize the data, etc. 
Ideally the data will be human readable.
Make use of subagents to solve the task.
